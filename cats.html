My cats :3

I have two cats named Salt and Misty!

Salt!

Salt's birthday is April 28th, 2022. I got her from my cousin's friend on June 24th, 2022, when she was two months old! Her mom was a pregnant stray who got adopted by my cousin's friend, and she has a twin sister named Pepper who lives with someone else.

Of the two, Salt is older by a few months, but she's also smaller... It's the older sibling curse.

We got Salt when I graduated, in exchange for me living at home and commuting.

Salt is quite attached to my younger brother. I'm probably her least favorite person in the house because I do all the scary things, like nail trims, meds, and vet visits :(

Fun fact: Salt knows how to open doors, even locked ones!

Misty!

Misty's birthday is June 1st, 2022. I got her off Craigslist on August 2nd, 2022, when she was 2 months old as well!

Misty is fluffy and big, and also quite stupid.

We got Misty because Salt had a lot of separation anxiety whenever I or my brother left her sight (she'd start crying when we went to shower), and we thought a sister might help. Salt and Misty coexist and tolerate each other, but they're not very close.

Misty is way closer to me than the rest of my family. She's my special little guy :)

Fun fact: Misty's favorite thing in the world is being chased by me! If I get tired and go up to my room before she's done, she screams.
